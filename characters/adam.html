ADAM SMASHER

Actor: mr_cheese225

Team: TEAM 1

Status: SAFE

HE IS ALWAYS WATCHING

Short Bio

Adam Smasher is unlike any other contestant in Anime Showdown Ultimax. More machine than man, he approaches every challenge with ruthless efficiency and almost no regard for the lives around him. While most contestants search for friendships, alliances, or a way home, Adam views the competition as nothing more than another assignment.

Contestants often describe an unsettling feeling whenever he enters a room. He rarely speaks, never wastes movement, and almost always appears to be observing someone. Whether he is studying his opponents... or reporting them... remains unknown.

Quote

"You look like a cut of fuckable meat."

How They Arrived

Before ASU

Adam Smasher was carrying out another Arasaka mission inside Night City. To him, it was no different from any other contract. Eliminate the target. Complete the objective. Get paid.

Moments before finishing his mission, reality itself began to distort. Buildings stretched unnaturally, communications were cut, and the familiar lights of Night City disappeared without warning.

Arrival

Unlike every other contestant, Adam did not awaken beside the others. He awoke completely alone.

Waiting for him was someone who introduced himself only as The Host. Rather than forcing Adam into the competition immediately, the Host offered him a single proposition.

Watch the contestants.
Report everything.
Never betray me.

In exchange, Adam would receive one wish. After several moments of silence... Adam accepted.

Current Story

Adam publicly presents himself as just another contestant, rarely speaking unless absolutely necessary. While others build alliances and friendships, Adam spends most of his time silently observing everyone around him.

Unknown to almost everyone on the island, Archive records suggest Adam's role extends far beyond simply competing in challenges.

The Exit Team currently considers Adam one of the biggest unknowns in the competition. Whether he is simply surviving... or serving someone else... remains one of the island's greatest mysteries.

Lore

WARNING: FILE CORRUPTION DETECTED.

Adam Smasher's Archive file contains more missing pages than nearly every other contestant currently participating in Anime Showdown Ultimax.

Several internal records reference conversations that officially do not exist. Multiple sections have been manually erased from the Archive, leaving only fragments behind.

During the recovered recording known as Hotlog 002, Adam entered into an agreement with the Host after being promised a single wish:

"Unlimited power.
To prove that metal surpasses organic life."

Since that meeting, every major movement made by the Exit Team has somehow reached the Host before it could be carried out.

Whether Adam is responsible has never been confirmed.

The Host has never denied it.
