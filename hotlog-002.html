THE DEAL

[RECORDING STARTS]

[Sound: Heavy metallic footsteps.]

THE HOST: You're awake.

ADAM: Where the fuck am I?

THE HOST: Far from Night City.

ADAM: ...

THE HOST: Do you know why I brought you here?

ADAM: No.

THE HOST: Because I need someone who cannot be broken.

ADAM: Find someone else.

THE HOST: There isn't anyone else.

[Five seconds of silence.]

THE HOST: I have an offer.

ADAM: Not interested.

THE HOST: You haven't heard it.

ADAM: I don't have to.

THE HOST: Work for me. Watch them. Learn them. Tell me everything.

ADAM: Go fuck yourself.

[Silence.]

THE HOST: Interesting answer. Unfortunately... it wasn't a request.

ADAM: You think you can force me?

THE HOST: No. I think I can convince you.

ADAM: ...

THE HOST: Complete your task and I will grant you one wish. Anything.

[Nine seconds of silence.]

ADAM: Anything?

THE HOST: Anything.

ADAM: Then prove it.

THE HOST: What do you want?

ADAM: Unlimited power. I want to prove something. That metal beats organic. Every time. No exceptions.

THE HOST: ... A simple wish. Accepted.

ADAM: What's the catch?

THE HOST: You already know. You never betray me.

ADAM: And if I do?

THE HOST: ... You won't.

ADAM: Confident.

THE HOST: Not confidence. Knowledge.

THE HOST: I know every choice you'll make before you make it.

THE HOST: I know every lie you'll tell. Every betrayal you'll consider. Every doubt you'll have.

[Heartbeat detected.]

ADAM: ...

THE HOST: You already know about Joker. Law. Gyro. Frieza. David. The Exit Team.

THE HOST: You'll tell me what they're planning. Every time.

ADAM: ... Fine.

THE HOST: Good. One final thing. Do not grow attached to them. Contestants are replaceable.

ADAM: That wasnt an option for me.

THE HOST: You're dismissed.

[Silence.]

ADAM: ... You're one sick bastard.

THE HOST: I've been called worse.

[RECORDING TERMINATED]
