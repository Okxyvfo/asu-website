THE ESCAPE PLAN

[RECORDING STARTS]

LAW: We're done hiding.

JOKER: Nobody else knows?

GYRO: Nobody. Not a damn word.

DAVID: Then we do this tonight.

FRIEZA: Failure is not an option.

[Door opens silently.]

[No footsteps detected.]

THE HOST: Interesting.

DAVID: What the hell?!

LAW: How long have you been there?

THE HOST: Long enough.

JOKER: You weren't supposed to know.

THE HOST: Secrets only work when someone isn’t watching.

GYRO: We didn’t say anything outside this room.

THE HOST: Joker suggested the tunnels. Law planned the route. Gyro calculated the timing. Frieza would break the security systems. David would stay behind if needed.

[Silence: 00:12]

LAW: That’s impossible…

THE HOST: No. It’s expected.

FRIEZA: You think knowing our plan means you own us?

THE HOST: I don’t think. I know.

JOKER: Then why let us keep talking?

THE HOST: Because hope is better television than fear.

THE HOST: But I think… it’s time for a demonstration.

[Signal distortion intensifies]

THE HOST: Listen carefully.

THE HOST: You may plan. You may whisper. You may lie to the cameras. You may even believe you are free.

THE HOST: But this is my world.

[Sickening wet crunch]

LAW: Wha— AGHHH!!

THE HOST: (calmly) Take Law for example. I simply reach into his chest… and pull.

[Ripping flesh, snapping bones, spraying liquid]

LAW: GHHH—!!

THE HOST: His spine comes apart so easily. One half in my left hand… the other still twitching on the floor. Blood everywhere. It paints the walls in thick, dark arcs. He’s still conscious for a few seconds, you know. Still trying to use his Room. Cute.

[Gurgling, choking]

GYRO: You monster—!!

[Violent tearing sounds]

THE HOST: Gyro next. I twist his head 360 degrees… then keep going until the neck becomes red paste. The golden spin he’s so proud of never even activates.

DAVID: STOP!!

[Heavy impact, bones shattering]

THE HOST: David tries to be heroic. I crush his cybernetic spine like a soda can. The metal screams louder than he does.

FRIEZA: I’ll kill you—

[Sudden silence, then wet explosion]

THE HOST: Frieza’s regeneration is impressive… but not faster than me tearing him in half from the skull down. Golden blood mixes with the red. It’s almost artistic.

JOKER: (panting, broken) …This isn’t over…

THE HOST: (softly, almost kindly) Oh Joker… I already know exactly how you die too. But not today.

THE HOST: You all get to go back to pretending. Smile for the audience. Fight in my challenges. And remember that I heard this conversation… and ended it… before you even finished having it.

[RECORDING HEAVILY DAMAGED]
