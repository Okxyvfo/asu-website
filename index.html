Anime Showdown Ultimax is a cinematic anime reality competition where contestants battle through challenges, rivalries, betrayals, and elimination fights to become the ultimate champion.

The story scenes are scripted. The challenges are real. At the end of every episode, the two players with the most votes enter an Elimination Battle. One survives. One leaves.
